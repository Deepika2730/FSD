NAV BAR

In Bootstrap, the navigation bar (or navbar) is a component that provides a responsive and customizable way to create navigation menus for your website. The navbar typically contains links to different sections of your site, branding elements, and optionally, form controls, search bars, or other components. It helps users navigate between different pages or sections of your website easily.
