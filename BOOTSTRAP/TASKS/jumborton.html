JUMBORTON

a "jumbotron" is a large, prominent container that you can use to showcase key content or information on a web page. It's often used to grab the user's attention and provide a clear focal point for important messages, announcements, or introductory content. The jumbotron component helps create a visually appealing and engaging header section for your website.
