Button Styles

Default Buttons: These are standard, rectangular buttons with a simple appearance.


Primary Buttons: Typically used for the most important actions on a page. They have a prominent color.


Success, Warning, and Danger Buttons:These buttons are colored to indicate various types of alerts or actions. Success is typically green, warning is yellow, and danger is red.


Info Buttons: Used to convey informational actions. The color for info buttons is typically blue.

Link Buttons: These buttons look like links and are usually used to mimic navigation links. They have no background color and are underlined.


Button Group: Bootstrap allows you to group buttons together for related actions.
